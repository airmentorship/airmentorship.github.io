FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

I know nothing about AI, but I am interested. Is this program for me?

Absolutely! We jump-start anyone into their AI careers by providing study-groups, workshops, tutorials, and hands-on learning opportunities.

What languages and frameworks to I need to know?

One needs to know basic python for most of the projects that we cover, but worry not: Python is the first item covered in the syllabus

When are the meetings?

Our intent is for individual groups (i.e. study groups & project groups) to set up their own regular meeting times. Contact group Leaders for their regular meeting schedule. In addition to these meetings, we have class lessons scheduled every Saturday. The lab also has open meetings on thursdays with occassional guest speakers that give tech talks (if you want a platform to present your ideas/research, let us know and we might be able to arrange something). Be sure to check out the calendar tab for event details.

How much are dues? How do I pay them?

There are currently no dues for joining the class!

When does the next cohort begin?

Get in touch and we shall let ou know when we set a date. in the meantime, feel free to drop in on the lab's thursday discussions
